Privacy Policy

We collect flight numbers, airlines, and dates that you provide for tracking purposes. We also collect your Telegram chat ID for sending notifications.

Your information is used solely for providing flight tracking services and sending relevant notifications through Telegram.

Payment processing is handled through TON Connect. We do not store your cryptocurrency wallet information.

Your flight tracking data is stored securely and retained only for the duration necessary to provide our services.

We use third-party services for flight data and payment processing. These services have their own privacy policies.
